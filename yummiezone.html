Yummie Zone

Serving organizations a fine blend of tasty and yummy yum dishes!

Overview

Offers a centralized hub that provides a convenient and efficient way for employees to order food on a daily or weekly basis. Onboard numerous vendors into the system to provide the opportunity for diverse delicacies to employees.


Benefits of using this Solution

Onboard Food Vendors.
  • Onboard various food vendors into the system with ease.
Feedbacks.
  • Get feedback from employees to ensure that vendors maintain the highest level of credibility when it comes to food quality.
Order Foods.
  • Order food easily and conveniently from the list of available vendors.

In conclusion, Yummie Zone is the ultimate solution to all your food ordering needs within your organization. With our centralized hub, you can onboard various food vendors, order food conveniently, and provide feedback to ensure that there is the food provided is of quality standards!
