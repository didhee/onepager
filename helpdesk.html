Help Desk

Let's help you track and resolve your employees' worries, one ticket at a time!.

Overview

Welcome to our powerful Helpdesk solution - designed to keep track of all employee complaints across multiple channels in one place, enabling your help desk team to support employees better and faster. Our cloud-based help desk software provides real-time insight into the complaints received from employees and how your team is performing. With our feature-rich help desk, ticket resolution is sped up with the help of proactive automation and highly customizable workflows.

Our dual analytic dashboard is a game-changer. It enables the helpdesk administrator to manage tickets on both a departmental and agent-specific level, providing an analytical overview of all open tickets broken down by resolution status, category, priority, status, and department. This gives the administrator insight into a wide range of operational events, including which agents have the most open tasks, which tasks have been completed by agents, and much more.
