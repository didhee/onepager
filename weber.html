Overview

Introducing WeBer Pro - the ultimate carpooling solution for efficient and cost-effective business travel! Our 3-in-1 solution features WeBer, WeBer Expense App, and WeBer Admin, all working together seamlessly to ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience. Your employees can simply book and schedule a ride on WeBer, which will notify the designated driver of the pick-up location. Meanwhile, the Expense App tracks all expenses incurred during the trip, and our Admin dashboard provides oversight to managers in charge, ensuring everything runs smoothly.
But that's not all - WeBer, the employee version of WeBer Pro, offers additional features that make it a must-have for any business. With the ability to book, join, and manage trips, as well as rate drivers, WeBer takes the stress out of business travel.


Benefits of using this Solution

Improves utilization of organizations' fleet of cars
  • WeBer Pro helps organizations get the most out of their fleet of cars by optimizing usage and minimizing waste. With WeBer Pro, employees can easily book and schedule rides, ensuring that all available vehicles are being used efficiently. This helps to reduce downtime and ensure that vehicles are being used to their full potential. In addition, the analytics provided by WeBer Pro can help organizations track vehicle usage and identify any areas where improvements can be made. This helps to reduce costs and improve the overall efficiency of the fleet.
Allows organizations fully to utilize the features of Microsoft 365.
  • WeBer Pro is fully integrated with Microsoft 365, allowing organizations to maximize their investment in this powerful suite of productivity tools. This integration means that employees can use their existing Microsoft 365 credentials to log in to WeBer Pro, streamlining the login process and ensuring security. In addition, WeBer Pro makes use of Microsoft 365's powerful collaboration tools, allowing employees to easily communicate with each other and share information as needed. This integration ensures that organizations can take full advantage of all the features of Microsoft 365, while also providing a seamless user experience for employees.
Analytics that show all ratings that have been given to drivers.
  • WeBer Pro provides organizations with detailed analytics that show all ratings that have been given to drivers. This information is invaluable for organizations that are looking to ensure that their drivers are providing a high level of service to employees. With this information, organizations can identify any areas where improvements can be made and take corrective action as needed. In addition, the ratings system can help to motivate drivers to provide excellent service, as they know that their performance is being tracked and evaluated. This can help to improve the overall quality of service provided by the organization.

Save time and money by avoiding traffic and reducing expenses with WeBer Pro. Give your employees the gift of hassle-free business travel - try WeBer Pro today!
