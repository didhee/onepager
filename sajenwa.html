Overview

Sajenwa is a powerful application that allows Retail managers to maintain absolute control of all inventory and sales, maximize security and minimize employee fraud. Sajenwa unifies in-store sales and inventory in a single fold. It helps you easily manage sales transactions, on your laptop, even with your mobile devices when you are on the go.
