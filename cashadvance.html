Cash Advance

For proper accountability on cash requests and retirements!

Overview

Responsible for ensuring that all expenditures from a cash advance are documented appropriately. Caters for the request of employees' cash advance requests as well as the retirement of these requests. Provides administrators with a comprehensive report of all the cash advance that has been requested by all employees and the amount of money going in and out of the organization.


Benefits of using this Solution

Valuable Insights.
  • Get insights into the frequency and amount of cash advances requested by employees, so you can make informed decisions about your organization's financial future.
Proper Documentation of all Requests.
  • Proper documentation of all cash requests. This reduces the risk of lost documentation or unapproved expenses, so you can rest easy knowing that everything is accounted for.
Efficient Cash Disbursement.
  • Efficiency in the process of Cash Advance disbursement.
