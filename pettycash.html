Petty Cash

Keeping track of every petty expenses, employee by employee!.

Overview

Track and manage the disbursement of small expenses with the Petty Cash App. Simplifies the task of requesting and claiming cash, making it easier to keep a handle on the organization's cash flow.


Benefits of using this Solution

Cash Claim.
  • Track past expenses and provide insights on cash claims.
Approval Configuration.
  • Set a cash approval process to minimize the risk of fraud.
Speed up Cash Requests.
  • Swiftly request for small cash for organization expenditure.

  • (n) no. of PowerApps Per App License.
