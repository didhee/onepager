Overview

Assentory is the ultimate solution for effective asset maintenance management. Understanding the importance of keeping your assets in good operating condition, our cloud-based solution is designed to help you plan, document, report, and schedule maintenance activities on your assets with ease. Assentory solution keeps an inventory of assets, ensuring that you have full visibility into your assets' status, location, and maintenance history. This helps you to identify potential issues before they become problems, saving you time and money. With Assentory, you can effectively manage maintenance activities for assets in various industries such as facilities, plant equipment, production machinery, and more! Our platform is seated on a Power-Platform, providing you with an intuitive, easy-to-use interface that enables you to keep track of all maintenance activities, assign tasks to team members, and monitor progress in real-time.

Benefits of using this Solution

Improved efficiency (assets in good repair tend to operate better)
  • When your assets are in good repair, they tend to operate better, which means that they can work more efficiently. This increased efficiency can help you save on energy costs, reduce downtime, and improve overall productivity.
Less equipment downtime, fewer interruptions to critical operations, and longer asset life.
  • With our platform, you can monitor your assets' performance and schedule preventive maintenance activities to ensure that they operate at optimal levels. This approach can reduce equipment downtime, which means that there are fewer interruptions to critical operations. Additionally, the longer your assets operate at optimal levels, the longer their useful life will be, which means that you can get more value from your assets over time.
Increased workplace safety and improved compliance.
  • Keeping your assets in good working condition is crucial for workplace safety and compliance. Our platform helps you identify potential safety hazards and compliance issues, allowing you to address them before they become major problems. This approach ensures that your assets are safe to operate and that you comply with all applicable regulations.

Don't let unexpected asset downtime disrupt your operations - sign up for Assentory today and take your asset maintenance management to the next level!
