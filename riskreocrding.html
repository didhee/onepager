Overview

Are you tired of cumbersome and outdated risk management systems that make it difficult to capture, manage and track risk in your projects? Look no further! Our solution, Risk Recording, is here to help digitize and simplify your risk management processes, improving compliance and safety outcomes. With real-time data and analytics, proactive near-miss capture, and historical risk trending, Risk Recording gives you the tools you need to stay ahead of the curve and ensure the safety of your workforce.

Benefits of using this Solution

Real-time data and analytics.
  • One of the key benefits of Risk Recording is the real-time data and analytics it provides about your risk assessments and safety performance. You'll be able to see and track risks as they emerge, giving you the ability to take immediate action to mitigate them. This real-time feedback will enable you to make informed decisions about how to allocate resources and manage risk in your projects.
Capture near misses.
  • Another key advantage of Risk Recording is that it enables you to capture all near misses as a proactive means to avert real accidents. By capturing and analyzing these near misses, you can identify potential hazards before they become actual incidents, reducing the risk of harm to your workforce, and preventing costly project delays.
Risk reporting and tracking.
  • Risk Recording provides you with a good risk history for trending and project risk planning. By tracking historical data, you can identify trends and patterns in your risk management processes, enabling you to improve your risk assessments and plan for potential risks in future projects.
