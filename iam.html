Information Access Management

Stay updated with information and analysis on employees' device usage!

Overview

Information Access Management (IAM) is the smart system that provides your organization with unparalleled access to information about all organizational-given devices and their assets.
With IAM, you can easily retrieve information on the usage and management of devices by individual employees, helping you optimize your operations and improve productivity. Our cutting-edge technology allows you to track all of your assets in real time, giving you the data, you need to make informed decisions about your business.

Benefits of using this Solution

Benefits
  • Information Access Management (IAM) ensures that all devices issued to employees are adequately accounted for, monitored, and utilized in the right way. Our system tracks device usage by recording the last logged-in time retrieval, so you always know who is using your devices and when. Plus, you can track device locations to ensure that they are within approved geographic locations, providing an added layer of security for your organization.
Benefits
  • The Information and Access Management solution provide you with accurate insight into real statistics of employee productivity and engagements, both within and outside of work hours. This is especially useful in cases of overtime business engagements, allowing you to monitor productivity and ensure that employees are compensated appropriately.
Benefits
  • IAM solution makes it easy to onboard new employees and configure them instantly into the organizational Office 365 tenant. You can also disengage Office 365 accounts of former employees, streamlining your operations and reducing the risk of security breaches.

Overall, Information Access Management (IAM) is the ultimate solution for device management. Our user-friendly interface and intuitive platform make it easy for you to manage all your devices and activities, ensuring the security and productivity of your organization!
