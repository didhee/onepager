Information Access Management

Stay updated with information and analysis on employees' device usage!

Overview

The Information Access Management is a smart system that provides an organization with information on all organizational-given devices, and all their assets as well as retrieving information on its usage and management by individual employees. The use of Reliance Information Access Management for Asset Tracking further offers one-third of the capabilities of Microsoft Office 365 and Microsoft Dataverse.
