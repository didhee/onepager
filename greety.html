Greety

Keeping you updated with visitors' on-premise activities, digitally!

Overview

Imagine being able to effortlessly monitor every visit that takes place at your organization, while also gathering valuable data that can be used to improve your services. With the Greety, this is not only possible but easy and efficient. Greety is a modern Digital Receptionist that allows you manage your visitors with ease, ensuring that every visit is recorded and tracked. With a user-friendly interface, visitors can sign in and out quickly, while also providing important information such as their email address. This information can then be used by your organization to send notifications of services or promotions that may be of interest to your visitors, enhancing the customer experience and improving engagement.
But that's not all - the Greety provides valuable analytics that can help you improve your operations. With data on every visit, you can identify patterns and trends, such as peak visit times, and adjust your operations accordingly. This can help to optimize your resources, reduce wait times, and enhance the overall customer experience.

Benefits of using this Solution

Modernize your reception.
  • With our self-service desk, visitors can quickly and easily schedule a visit request and attend to their already-raised visit request, reducing the need for a receptionist to create a paper log to capture visitors' entries. This modernized approach not only saves time but also enhances the visitor experience by providing a more efficient process.
Notify your staff.
  • But that's not all - the Greety also provides the ability to notify your staff quickly and easily through a powerful workflow capacitated by Power Automate. Whether it's through email or SMS, our system ensures that staff members are kept informed of visitors' activities and requests, with handy features such as approval via email.
Analysis.
  • Finally, with the Greety's analysis capabilities, you can analyze visitors' data generated to identify opportunities to create optimization. With insights into visitor patterns and trends, you can optimize your resources, reduce wait times, and enhance the overall visitor experience.

Say goodbye to manual receptionist systems and hello to the future of visitor management with the Greety. With modernized processes, enhanced communication, and powerful security features, it's the perfect solution for any organization looking to take its visitor management to the next level. Try the Greety today and experience the benefits for yourself!
