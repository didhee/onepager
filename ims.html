Information Management System

Stay updated with information and analysis on employees' device usage!

Overview

A smart system that provides your organization with unparalleled access to information about all organizational-given devices and their assets. Easily retrieve information on the usage and management of devices by individual employees. Track all your assets in real-time, giving you the data, you need to make informed decisions about your business.

Benefits of using this Solution

Track Employee Device Usage.
  • Ensure all devices issued to employees are adequately accounted for, monitored, and utilized in the right way.
Onboard New Employees.
  • Easily onboard new employees and configure them instantly into the organizational Office 365 tenant. Disengage Office 365 accounts of former employees, streamlining your operations and reducing the risk of security breaches.
Insight into Employees' Productivity.
  • Track and manage employees' activities on OneDrive, Teams, Email, SharePoint, etc. to get accurate insight into real statistics of employee productivity and engagements.

Overall, Information Management System(IMS) is the ultimate solution for device management. Our user-friendly interface and intuitive platform make it easy for you to manage all your devices and activities, ensuring the security and productivity of your organization!
